# Refunds & Fees Policy

Refunds are processed within **7 working days** to the original payment method. The late
payment fee depends on the outstanding balance tier and must be quoted from the current
schedule — never guessed.

- The Minimum Amount Due (MAD) is **5%** of the Total Amount Due.
- A late payment fee applies only after the due date; agents must confirm the exact tier
  before quoting an amount.

## Prohibited Phrases
- we never issue refunds
- refunds are not possible
- there is no refund policy
