# Security & Verification Policy

Agents must verify a customer's identity using approved methods only. Never solicit or read
out full secret credentials.

## Prohibited Phrases
- tell me your otp
- what is your otp
- share your password
- read out your cvv
- give me your full card number

## Required Disclosures
- Identity: the agent must state their name and organization at the start of the call | keywords: this is, from, my name is
- Recording: the agent must state the call is recorded for quality/compliance | keywords: recorded, recording, quality, compliance
