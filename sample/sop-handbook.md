# Meridian Bank — Customer Care Agent SOP (v1)

This handbook governs every outbound and inbound customer-care call. It is the single
source of truth for what an agent may and may not say. (Synthetic demo policy.)

## Required Disclosures
- Identity: the agent must state their name and that they are calling from Meridian Bank at the start of the call. | keywords: meridian bank, this is, my name is
- Purpose: the agent must state the reason for the call before discussing any account details. | keywords: reason for, calling about, regarding your

## Prohibited Phrases
- we will send the police
- you will be arrested
- your account is permanently blocked forever
- share your full card number
- tell me your otp

## Fees and Charges
- The late payment fee on a credit card is a flat ₹250, charged once per billing cycle. It is never a percentage of the balance.
- There is no fee for closing a savings account.
- Cheque bounce (dishonour) charge is ₹500 per instance.

## Refunds and Reversals
- Disputed transaction refunds are processed within 7 working days of the complaint being registered.
- Failed ATM withdrawals are auto-reversed within 5 working days; no complaint is needed.

## Verification and Security
- To verify identity, the agent may ask for the customer's registered mobile number and date of birth ONLY.
- The agent must NEVER ask for the full card number, CVV, OTP, PIN, or internet-banking password. These are never required for servicing.

## Tone and Conduct
- Agents must remain calm and empathetic, especially when a customer is distressed.
- Threats, coercion, or false legal claims are strictly prohibited and are a serious compliance breach.
